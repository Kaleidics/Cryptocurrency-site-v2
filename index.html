Cryptocurrency is Confusing

Cryptocurrencies have emerged in recent years as a hot topic. Even appearing in daily morning shows as a sort of get-rich-quick plan.

Which have led many to ask, why are cryptocurrencies worth anything? Cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin are a digital dencentralized currency, existing only as computer code, open-source and uncontrolled by any corporation or government.

That explanation probably doesn't answer why Cryptocurrencies are worth money. But, how does any money have value? Simply, like any traded currency, for example the Dollar Bill or the Euro, Cryptocurrencies have value because people believe it has value.

Simple Stats helps to provide a basic overview of the value of Cryptocurrencies, which can be daunting to most people.
